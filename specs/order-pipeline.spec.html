Order pipeline rework

specs / order-pipeline.spec.html

Goals

Rework the order pipeline so a checkout completes in under two seconds at p95, without dropping orders during fulfillment-service restarts.

Non-goal: changing the public checkout API surface.

Non-goal: changing the database schema during this work.

Latency budget

Current p95 per stage, measured over the last 14 days.

Inventory p95 is cache-miss dominated.

Bringing enqueue under the 800 ms target is follow-up work owned by the fulfillment team.

Retry policy

Enqueue retries three times with exponential backoff and full jitter, then dead-letters the order.

Dead-lettered orders page the fulfillment on-call rotation via PagerDuty and post to #orders-dlq.

Nothing is silently dropped; the DLQ runbook owns recovery for dead-lettered orders.
